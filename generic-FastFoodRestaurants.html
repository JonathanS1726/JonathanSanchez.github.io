Fast Food Restaurant Analysis

This time I am exploring the world of Tableau to analyze the US fast-food restaurants dataset.

There are hundreds of Fast food restaurants across the US and some of them have more outlets than others. Feel free to peek into this simple yet insightful dashboard and to know a little more about your favorite restaurant.

As I took the time to explore and clean the data in SQL Server I was able to find some duplicates and misspelled names within this dataset. Keep in mind that data that is not properly cleaned could potentially be translated to wrong decision-making by anyone using or consuming your reports.
