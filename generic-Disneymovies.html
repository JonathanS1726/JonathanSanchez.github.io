After Analyzing this data I have found that there were not as many movies produced by Disney up until the mid-'80s. Only 45 Movies were released during this time frame in which Adventure and Musical were the predominant genres. However, even though only around 2 billion dollars gross were collected from these productions we can see that the adjusted gross reached the amount of nearly 18 billion dollars with an increment adjusted gross of 1,157% by 1985. Pretty impressive right?

When it comes to MPAA rating we've found that PG is at the top of the list between the first movie released in 1937 till 2016, followed by PG-13 which proves that most of the movies produced by #Disney are not 100% directed to the little ones.

Moving to the top-grossing movies by Genre tab (page 2) you will find that The Avengers is at the top of the list under the Adventure Genre with 936 million Dollars, speaking of which, Adventure is the Genre leader with nearly $16bn total gross overall.
