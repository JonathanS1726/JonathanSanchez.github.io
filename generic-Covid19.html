COVID-19 Live Report

*You might need to create a Microsoft PBI account to view this report

In this report, I analyze COVID-19 Pandemic spread around the world in an updated and interactive Power BI Report.

Navigate to the Worldwide tab (page 1) to find the most cases by Country, while having a total count of the Active cases and total Deaths. You can also filter by Country and find a summary of the top 15 most affected countries on the left side pane.

The Country by Country Analysis tab (page 2) will give more detailed information on the cases of each country based on your selection. Recovery rate and Mortality rate gauges will show how some countries have been showing progress when fighting the virus against the rate of people that have lost the fight ever since the beginning of the spread.

If you need more information about the COVID-19 and the source of this data, head to the Learn More tab (page 3) and educate yourself, so we can all do our part in fighting this virus that has cost so many lives already.
